Privacy Policy

Monty Math is built to be simple, private, and respectful of your data.

The short version: The Monty Math app collects, stores, and shares no personal data — it runs entirely on your device, with no accounts and no tracking. This website uses simple, privacy-friendly analytics (visitor counts and page views) that set no cookies and identify no one.

1. The App Collects No Data

The Monty Math app does not collect any personally identifiable information. Specifically:

  • No account creation required
  • No name, email address, or contact information collected
  • No location tracking
  • No equations or inputs stored or transmitted
  • No third-party analytics or advertising SDKs in the app

2. How the App Works

All functionality in Monty Math — the Algebra Engine, Unit Circle, and Scientific Notation tools — operates entirely on your device. No data is transmitted to any server or third party. Your inputs exist only in your device's memory while the app is running.

3. Website Analytics

This website (montymath.com) uses GoatCounter, a privacy-friendly analytics tool, to understand basic usage — such as how many people visit and which pages are popular. It is designed to respect your privacy:

  • No cookies are set, and no tracking happens across other websites
  • No personal data is collected, and individual visitors are not identified or profiled
  • Your IP address is used only momentarily to count the visit and is never stored
  • The data is not sold or shared, and is used only to improve the site

These analytics apply to the website only — the app itself remains entirely data-free, as described above.

4. Third-Party Services

Monty Math is distributed through the Apple App Store and Google Play Store. These platforms may collect certain usage and device information in accordance with their own privacy policies:

  • Apple's Privacy Policy →
  • Google's Privacy Policy →

5. Children's Privacy

Monty Math is designed for high school students and does not knowingly collect personal information from anyone, including children under the age of 13. Because the app collects no data whatsoever, it is safe for use by students of all ages.

6. Changes to This Policy

If privacy practices ever change — for example if optional features involving data collection are introduced — this page will be updated and the date at the top changed. Data collection will never be introduced without clearly disclosing it here first.
