Privacy Policy

We built Heron to be simple, private, and respectful of your data. Here's exactly what that means.

The short version: Heron does not collect, store, or share any personal data. The app works entirely on your device. We have no user accounts, no tracking, and no analytics.

1. Information We Collect

Heron does not collect any personally identifiable information. Specifically:

  • We do not require you to create an account
  • We do not collect your name, email address, or any contact information
  • We do not track your location
  • We do not store any equations or inputs you enter into the app
  • We do not use any third-party analytics or advertising SDKs

2. How the App Works

All functionality in Heron — the Algebra Engine, Unit Circle, and Scientific Notation tools — operates entirely on your device. No data is transmitted to our servers or any third party. Your inputs exist only in your device's memory while the app is running and are not saved or transmitted anywhere.

3. Third-Party Services

Heron is distributed through the Apple App Store and Google Play Store. These platforms may collect certain usage and device information in accordance with their own privacy policies. We encourage you to review:

  • Apple's Privacy Policy →
  • Google's Privacy Policy →

We have no control over and assume no responsibility for the content, privacy policies, or practices of these third-party services.

4. Children's Privacy

Heron is designed for high school students and does not knowingly collect personal information from anyone, including children under the age of 13. Because the app collects no data whatsoever, it is safe for use by students of all ages.

5. Changes to This Policy

If we ever change our privacy practices — for example if we introduce optional features that involve data collection — we will update this page and change the "last updated" date at the top. We will never introduce data collection without clearly disclosing it here.
